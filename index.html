About

Who is
xFrightfull?

I'm a 17-year-old web developer from Azerbaijan who started building on the web because I wanted to create things that actually exist — not just code exercises, but real platforms real people use.

Everything I do falls under CubeNest Studios — my personal creative studio. Its first and flagship product is CubeNest: a Minecraft-focused hub for mods, tools, and community resources. It's my biggest project to date, and it's still growing.

I'm at a stage where every line of code teaches me something. I don't just learn — I ship. I believe the fastest way to grow is to build things in the open, fail, fix, and try again. That's the only approach that has ever worked for me.
